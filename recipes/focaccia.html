Focaccia

Crispy and easy to make focaccia

Focaccia is a delicious bread made with plenty of olive oil. it is flattened, almost like a pizza, but has a very light and airy core.

Ingredients

Sponge

  • ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast

Final dough

  • all Sponge
  • 3 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ⅓ cups water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ tablespoon salt

Toppings

  • 4 to 6 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Olive oil, to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl add ¾ cups all-purpose flour and 1 teaspoon active dry yeast and stir with a spoon, add ½ cup water and stir until uniform, Cover with a damp cloth or plastic wrap and let rise for 30 to 60 minutes until doubled in volume.
  2. In a bigger bowl add 3 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour, ½ tablespoon salt, all the Sponge, 1 ⅓ cups water and 2 tablespoons olive oil stir until the dough become sticky. cover an let rest 5 minutes.
  3. With your hands wet pull the sides of the dough to the center 4 or 5 times and let rest 5 minutes
  4. Repeat the last step and let rest 5 minutes
  5. Keep your hands wet and slide under the center of the dough and pull up until one of the sides pull away from the bowl, roll it under the dough, repeat this process for the other side. Repeat this process with the opposite sides 3 times. cover and let rest 5 minutes
  6. Repeat the last step and let rest 30 to 60 minutes until doubled in volume.
  7. Pre heat the oven at 390 F
  8. spread olive oil on the bottom of the baking tray and Stretch the dough until it is at max 2 inches.
  9. Put the toppings to the taste
  10. Bake for 25 minutes or until brown
