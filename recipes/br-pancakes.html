Brazilian Pancake

Fluffy Brazilian Style Pancakes

Easy and quick-to-make Brazilian pancakes, ideal for breakfast or a quick snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup all purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup milk

Steps

  1. Combine egg, sugar and butter, stir until mixture become uniform, add milk, flour and baking powder and stir until uniform again.
  2. Put a pan in low heat and let it warm for a while
  3. Put some of the dough in the pan (approximately 1/2 cup) and let it cook until the side that is in contact with the pan starts to brown
  4. Flip the pancake to cook uniformly and wait until brown.
